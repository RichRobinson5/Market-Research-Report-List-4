<p><strong>The Ribbon Fiber Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ribbon fiber cables are available in two main types, single-mode and multi-mode. Single-mode ribbon fiber cables are designed for long-distance communication with a single pathway for light signals, offering high bandwidth and low signal attenuation. Multi-mode ribbon fiber cables are used for shorter distances with multiple pathways for light signals, making them ideal for high-speed data transmission within buildings or campuses. Both types of ribbon fiber cables are commonly used in telecommunications and data networking applications.</p></p>
