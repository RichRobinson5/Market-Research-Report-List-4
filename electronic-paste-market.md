<p><ul><li>Conductive Paste</li><li>Resistance Paste</li><li>Dielectric Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic paste market consists of three main types - conductive paste, resistance paste, and dielectric paste. Conductive paste is used to create electrical connections in electronic circuits, resistance paste is used to create resistors in circuits, and dielectric paste is used to insulate components from each other. These pastes play a vital role in the manufacturing of electronic devices by enabling the flow of electricity, controlling resistance levels, and preventing short circuits. Each type of paste serves a unique function in the production of electronic components.</p></p>
