The second type ranges from 1.1-3.5 g/10 min, offering a moderate flow rate suitable for injection molding processes. The third type falls within the 3.6-5 g/10 min range, providing a higher flow rate for applications requiring faster processing times. These different MFR categories cater to a wide range of industrial needs and processes.</p></p>
