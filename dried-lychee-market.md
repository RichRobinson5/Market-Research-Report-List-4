<p><p>Dried Lychee market types can be categorized based on processing methods and appearance. By processing method, there are Baked Dried and Freeze Dried lychees. Baked Dried lychees are dried in an oven or dehydrator, while Freeze Dried lychees are frozen and then dehydrated. By appearance, lychees are categorized as Shelled or Unhulled. Shelled lychees have the outer skin removed before drying, while Unhulled lychees are dried with the skin intact. These different types cater to varying preferences and uses in the market.</p></p>
