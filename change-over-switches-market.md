<p><p>Change over switches are commonly categorized into two market types: automatic and manual. Automatic change over switches seamlessly transfer power supply from the main source to a backup source during power outages without requiring human intervention. Manual change over switches, on the other hand, require the user to physically switch between power sources. Both types of change over switches are essential for maintaining uninterrupted power supply in various applications such as residential buildings, commercial establishments, and industrial facilities.</p></p>
