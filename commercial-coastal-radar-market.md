<p><p>Commercial Coastal Radars can be categorized into two main types: X-Band Radar and S-Band Radar. X-Band Radar systems operate at higher frequencies and are best suited for short to medium range applications, offering high resolution for precise target detection. On the other hand, S-Band Radar systems operate at lower frequencies, providing longer range capabilities and better penetration through adverse weather conditions. Both types of radars play a crucial role in maritime surveillance, navigation, and security applications along coastal regions.</p></p>
