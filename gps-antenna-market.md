<p><strong>The GPS Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Polarization</li><li>Circular Polarization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS antenna market includes various types such as vertical polarization and circular polarization antennas. Vertical polarization antennas transmit signals with an electric field parallel to the Earth's surface, providing consistent coverage in urban environments with tall buildings. Circular polarization antennas emit signals in a spiral pattern, allowing for better reception in areas with obstacles or signal interference. Both types of antennas play crucial roles in improving satellite communication and location tracking accuracy in different environments and conditions.</p></p>
