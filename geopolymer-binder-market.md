<p><p>Geopolymer binder market can be classified into four main types based on the raw materials used: slag-based, rock-based, fly ash-based, and ferro-sialate-based. Slag-based binders utilize industrial by-products like blast furnace slag, rock-based binders incorporate natural minerals and rocks, fly ash-based binders use fly ash from coal combustion, and ferro-sialate-based binders use a combination of iron-rich materials. Each type of geopolymer binder offers unique properties and characteristics that cater to different applications and industries.</p></p>
