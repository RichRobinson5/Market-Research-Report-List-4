<p><p>Paperboard packaging can be categorized into two main types: Chipboard and Laminated/Pasted Chipboard. Chipboard, also known as paperboard, is a thick, rigid paper-based material commonly used for packaging boxes, displays, and other products. Laminated or pasted chipboard refers to chipboard that has been coated with a layer of laminate or paste for added strength, protection, and graphic enhancement. These types of paperboard packaging are widely used in the food and beverage, cosmetics, pharmaceutical, and consumer goods industries.</p></p>
