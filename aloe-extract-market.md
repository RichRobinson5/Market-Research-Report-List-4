<p><p>Aloe extract market is segmented into two main types: Aloe Gel Extracts and Aloe Whole Leaf Extracts. Aloe Gel Extracts are derived from the inner leaf of the aloe plant and contain a high concentration of the gel-like substance known for its moisturizing and healing properties. On the other hand, Aloe Whole Leaf Extracts include the entire leaf of the aloe plant, including the outer leaf, which contains additional beneficial compounds such as antioxidants and anti-inflammatory agents. Both types are used in various industries such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverages.</p></p>
