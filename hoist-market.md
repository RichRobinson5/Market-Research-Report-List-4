<p><p>Hoists are commonly categorized into four main types based on their power source: manual hoists, electric hoists, air hoists, and hydraulic hoists. Manual hoists require physical effort from the operator to lift and lower loads. Electric hoists use electricity to power the lifting mechanism, making them efficient and easy to operate. Air hoists utilize compressed air to lift heavy loads, ideal for industrial settings. Hydraulic hoists rely on hydraulic pressure to lift objects smoothly and steadily, commonly used in construction and automotive industries.</p></p>
