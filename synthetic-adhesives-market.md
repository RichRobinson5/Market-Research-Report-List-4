<p><p>Synthetic adhesives market can be categorized into four main types: Thermoplastic adhesives, Elastomer adhesives, Thermoset adhesives, and Others. Thermoplastic adhesives are capable of being softened by heat and hardening upon cooling, making them reversible. Elastomer adhesives are flexible and have high elongation properties. Thermoset adhesives undergo a chemical reaction during curing, providing strong and durable bonds. The others category includes specialty adhesives like UV-curable, pressure-sensitive, and structural adhesives tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
