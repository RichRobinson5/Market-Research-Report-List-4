<p><p>Nonfat Dry milk (NFDM) is available in two main market types: Skimmed Milk and Whole Milk. Skimmed Milk NFDM is made by removing all the fat from milk, resulting in a product with very low fat content. This type of NFDM is popular among health-conscious consumers looking for a lower-fat alternative to regular milk. Conversely, Whole Milk NFDM retains all the natural fat content of milk, providing a richer flavor and creamier texture preferred by those who enjoy the taste and mouthfeel of whole milk.</p></p>
