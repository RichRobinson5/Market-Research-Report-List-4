On the other hand, four-wheeled tractors are larger and more powerful machines suitable for heavy-duty agricultural tasks such as plowing, planting, and harvesting on large farms. Both types of tractors play important roles in modern agriculture, catering to different needs and scales of farming operations.</p></p>
