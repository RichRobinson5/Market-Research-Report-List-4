<p><p>X-Ray Photoelectron Spectrometers (XPS) are used for surface analysis of materials. In the monochromatic market, XPS instruments use a single wavelength of X-ray to excite electrons and provide high resolution spectra, allowing for precise identification of elements present. On the other hand, in the non-monochromatic market, XPS instruments use a broader range of X-ray wavelengths, offering faster analysis but with lower resolution. Both types of XPS instruments have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
