<p><ul><li>Root-form Dental Implants</li><li>Plate-form Dental Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tapered dental implants are categorized into two main types: root-form dental implants and plate-form dental implants. Root-form implants are shaped like a tooth root and are commonly used in cases where there is enough bone to support the implant. Plate-form implants have a flat and wide shape, making them suitable for cases where the jawbone is narrow or thin. Both types of implants offer a stable foundation for dental prosthetics and can provide long-lasting results for patients in need of tooth replacement.</p></p>
