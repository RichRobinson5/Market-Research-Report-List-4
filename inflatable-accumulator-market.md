<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>Spring-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the inflatable accumulator market, two common types are gravity and spring-type accumulators. Gravity accumulators use the force of gravity to store and release hydraulic energy, making them suitable for applications where a constant force is needed. Spring-type accumulators, on the other hand, use a spring mechanism to store energy, providing a more controlled and predictable release. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific needs of the system they are being used in.</p></p>
