<p><p>The Caustic Paint Remover market includes two main types: Oily and Paste. Oily paint removers are typically liquid-based and work by breaking down paint through the use of solvents. They are often easier to apply and can be effective on a variety of surfaces. On the other hand, paste paint removers are thicker in consistency and are applied like a paste onto the surface. They are often preferred for vertical surfaces as they can adhere better and provide a longer contact time for removing paint.</p></p>
