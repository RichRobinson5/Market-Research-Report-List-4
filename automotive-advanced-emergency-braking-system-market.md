<p><p>Automotive Advanced Emergency Braking Systems can be categorized into two main types: initiative and passive. Initiative systems are proactive in nature, using sensors and real-time data to detect potential collisions and automatically engage the braking system to prevent accidents. On the other hand, passive systems rely on the driver to initiate braking when a collision is imminent. While both types aim to increase safety on the roads, initiative systems are seen as more advanced and effective in preventing accidents.</p></p>
