<p><ul><li>Manual Pressurization</li><li>Semi-Automatic Pressurization</li><li>Automatic Pressurization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure paint pots are available in three main market types based on the method of pressurization: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. In manual pressurization, the user manually pressurizes the pot using a pump or valve. In semi-automatic pressurization, the pot is partially pressurized by the user, with the rest being automated. In automatic pressurization, the pot is fully pressurized automatically without any manual intervention. Each type offers different levels of convenience and efficiency for users in various painting applications.</p></p>
