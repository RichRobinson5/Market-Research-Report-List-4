<p><ul><li>High-density Polyethylene</li><li>Linear low-density Polyethylene</li><li>Low-density Polyethylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene plastic compounding market includes three main types: high-density polyethylene (HDPE), linear low-density polyethylene (LLDPE), and low-density polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE is known for its high strength and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications such as packaging and construction materials. LLDPE offers flexibility and impact resistance, commonly used in film and injection molding. LDPE, with its low tensile strength and clarity, is often used for packaging and tubing. These three types cater to different industry needs and applications within the plastic compounding market.</p></p>
