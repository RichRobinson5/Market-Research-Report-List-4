<p><ul><li>ON/OFF</li><li>PID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heat Treatment Temperature Control Equipment market consists of two main types of temperature control systems: ON/OFF and PID. ON/OFF systems simply turn the heating elements on or off based on whether the temperature is above or below a set point. PID systems, on the other hand, continuously adjust the heating element based on the difference between the desired temperature and the actual temperature. This allows for more precise control and more stable temperatures, making PID systems more commonly used in industries where accuracy is crucial.</p></p>
