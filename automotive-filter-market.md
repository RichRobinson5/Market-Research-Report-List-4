<p><p>The automotive filter market consists of three main types: fuel filter, engine air filter, and oil filter. Fuel filters remove contaminants from the fuel before it reaches the engine, ensuring proper combustion. Engine air filters prevent dust and debris from entering the engine, which can cause damage. Oil filters help to remove impurities from the engine oil, protecting the engine from wear and tear. These filters are essential components in maintaining the performance and longevity of a vehicle's engine.</p></p>
