<p><ul><li>Autograft</li><li>Allograft</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organ transplantation market includes three main types: autograft, allograft, and others. Autograft involves transplanting tissue from one part of a patient's body to another. Allograft involves transplanting tissue or organs from a donor to a recipient. The "others" category encompasses xenografts (transplants between species), synthetic grafts, and living donor transplants. Each type of transplantation has unique benefits and risks, making them suitable for different medical situations and patient needs.</p></p>
