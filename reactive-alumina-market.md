<p><ul><li>Powdered Form Reactive Alumina</li><li>Sphered Form Reactive Alumina</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reactive Alumina market can be divided into two main types based on their physical form: Powdered Form Reactive Alumina and Sphered Form Reactive Alumina. Powdered Form Reactive Alumina is finely ground to a powder, making it suitable for various industrial applications such as catalyst support and adsorbents. On the other hand, Sphered Form Reactive Alumina is formed into spherical beads, which are commonly used in high-performance abrasives and refractories. Both types offer unique characteristics and advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
