<p><ul><li>In-ear Earbud</li><li>Bone Conduction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swimming headphones come in two main types - in-ear earbuds and bone conduction headphones. In-ear earbuds are designed to fit snugly inside the ear canal, providing a secure fit and good sound quality underwater. Bone conduction headphones, on the other hand, sit just in front of the ear and transmit sound through vibrations in the cheekbones, leaving the ear canal open. Both types of headphones are waterproof and ideal for use while swimming, providing swimmers with options based on their preferences and comfort.</p></p>
