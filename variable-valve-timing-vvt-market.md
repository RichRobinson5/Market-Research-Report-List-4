<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) systems are designed to optimize engine performance by adjusting the timing of the opening and closing of the intake and exhaust valves. There are two main types of VVT systems in the market: Continuous VVT and Non-continuous VVT. Continuous VVT systems continuously adjust the valve timing throughout the engine's operating range, offering more precise control. Non-continuous VVT systems, on the other hand, only adjust the valve timing at specific RPM ranges, providing a simpler and more cost-effective solution for improving engine efficiency.</p></p>
