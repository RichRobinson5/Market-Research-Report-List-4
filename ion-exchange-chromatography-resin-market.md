<p><p>Ion exchange chromatography resin market includes cationic resin and anionic resin. Cationic resin contains positively charged functional groups that attract and exchange negatively charged ions in a sample, while anionic resin contains negatively charged functional groups that attract and exchange positively charged ions. Both types of resins are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and environmental analysis for separating and purifying compounds based on their ionic properties. These resins play a crucial role in the purification process in chromatography techniques.</p></p>
