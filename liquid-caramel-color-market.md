<p><p>Liquid caramel color is classified into four main types: plain caramel color, caustic sulfite caramel color, ammonia caramel color, and ammonia sulfite caramel color. </p><p>Plain caramel color is produced by heating sugar in the presence of acid or alkali, while caustic sulfite caramel color is made with sulfite compounds. Ammonia caramel color is produced using ammonia as a reactant, and ammonia sulfite caramel color combines both ammonia and sulfite compounds in its production process. Each type of caramel color offers different characteristics and applications in the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
