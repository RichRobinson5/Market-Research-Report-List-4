<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Airborne Digital Mapping Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Array Scanners (Pushbroom)</li><li>Area Arrays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear Array Scanners, also known as Pushbroom scanners, capture images by scanning a line of pixels across the ground as the aircraft moves forward. This type of mapping camera is typically used for large-scale mapping projects due to its high data collection rate. </p><p>On the other hand, Area Array cameras capture images using a two-dimensional pixel array, allowing for simultaneous capture of an entire image frame. This type of mapping camera is commonly used for detailed and high-resolution mapping applications. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in capturing accurate data for aerial mapping projects.</p></p>
