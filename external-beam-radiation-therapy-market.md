<p><p>External Beam Radiation Therapy market includes three main types: Conformal Radiation Therapy, Proton Beam Therapy, and Image Guided Radiation Therapy. Conformal Radiation Therapy uses computer technology to precisely shape the radiation beams to match the tumor's shape. Proton Beam Therapy utilizes high-energy protons to target tumors with minimal damage to surrounding healthy tissue. Image Guided Radiation Therapy combines imaging technology with radiation therapy to accurately locate and target tumors during treatment. These technologies offer more precise and effective cancer treatment options for patients.</p></p>
