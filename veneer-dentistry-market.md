<p><p>Veneers in dentistry are thin shells placed on the surface of teeth to improve their appearance. There are two main types of veneer materials - composite and dental porcelain. Composite veneers are made of a tooth-colored resin material that is applied directly on the teeth. Dental porcelain veneers are custom-made ceramic shells that are bonded to the teeth. Both types of veneers can effectively cover imperfections such as discoloration, chips, or misalignment, giving patients a brighter and more attractive smile.</p></p>
