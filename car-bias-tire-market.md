<p><p>Car Bias Tire Market Types can be categorized into two main groups: Ordinary Bias Tires and Bias Belted Tires. Ordinary Bias Tires are traditional tires constructed with overlapping ply layers at an angle to the bead, providing durability and stability at high speeds. On the other hand, Bias Belted Tires feature an extra layer of belts made of steel or other materials between the plies, enhancing strength and reducing deformation. Both types cater to different requirements and preferences of car owners in terms of performance and handling.</p></p>
