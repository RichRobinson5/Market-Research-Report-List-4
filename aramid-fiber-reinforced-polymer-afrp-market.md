<p><p>Aramid Fiber Reinforced Polymer (AFRP) is a type of composite material where aramid fibers are embedded in a polymer matrix. There are two main types of AFRP based on the polymer matrix used: Thermoplastic AFRP and Thermoset AFRP. Thermoplastic AFRP allows for reshaping and recycling due to its reversible chemical structure, making it suitable for applications requiring flexibility. On the other hand, Thermoset AFRP offers higher stiffness and strength due to its permanent cross-linked structure, making it ideal for applications requiring durability and heat resistance.</p></p>
