They come in different types based on their numerical aperture (NA), including NA=0.09, NA=0.065, NA=0.05, and others. A higher NA value indicates a wider light acceptance angle, allowing for better collimation. These different NA values cater to various applications with specific requirements for beam collimation, ensuring optimal performance in laser systems.</p></p>
