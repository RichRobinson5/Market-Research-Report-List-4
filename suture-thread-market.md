<p><ul><li>Short-term Absorbable</li><li>Mid-term Absorbable</li><li>Long-term Absorbable</li><li>Non-Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Suture thread market types are categorized based on their absorbability and longevity. Short-term absorbable sutures are typically made from materials that break down within weeks, suitable for quick-healing wounds. Mid-term absorbable sutures dissolve within a few months, ideal for wounds needing extended support. Long-term absorbable sutures can last for years before dissolving, often used in deep tissues. Non-absorbable sutures do not break down and must be removed manually, commonly used in surgeries where long-term support is required.</p></p>
