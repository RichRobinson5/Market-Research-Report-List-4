<p><ul><li>Casting Molding Solid Surface</li><li>Extrusion Molding Solid Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic solid surface market can be classified into two types: casting molding solid surface and extrusion molding solid surface. Casting molding involves pouring liquid acrylic material into a mold to create a solid surface with various designs and textures. On the other hand, extrusion molding uses a continuous process where acrylic material is forced through a die to create solid surfaces of consistent thickness and quality. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences in terms of aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
