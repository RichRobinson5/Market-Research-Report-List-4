<p><p>Zinc Oxide Non-linear Resistors (ZNR) are available in two main market types: SMD Type and Leaded Type. SMD Type ZNRs are surface mount devices that can be directly mounted onto circuit boards, offering space-saving advantages. Leaded Type ZNRs, on the other hand, come with lead wires for easy connection to circuits. Both types of ZNRs provide non-linear resistance characteristics, making them ideal for protecting circuits from voltage surges and overloads. They are commonly used in a variety of electronic devices and equipment.</p></p>
