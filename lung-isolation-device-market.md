<p><p>The Lung Isolation Device market consists of two main types: Endobronchial Blocker and Lung Dissociation Kit. Endobronchial Blockers are used to occlude one lung during surgical procedures, allowing ventilation of the other lung. Lung Dissociation Kits are used in research settings to isolate and study specific cell populations within the lung. Both devices play a critical role in medical and research settings by providing precise and controlled isolation of lung tissue for various purposes.</p></p>
