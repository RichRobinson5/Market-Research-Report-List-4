<p><p>Commercial milkshake machines are available in two main types: single spindle and multiple spindle. Single spindle machines are typically smaller and only able to make one milkshake at a time, while multiple spindle machines have the capacity to make multiple milkshakes simultaneously. Single spindle machines are often more compact, making them suitable for smaller businesses or establishments with lower demand. On the other hand, multiple spindle machines are ideal for high-volume environments such as busy cafes or ice cream shops where efficiency is key.</p></p>
