<p><p>Spark machining, also known as electrical discharge machining (EDM), includes three main market types: Sinker EDM, Wire EDM, and Hole Drilling EDM. Sinker EDM uses a shaped electrode to create a cavity in the workpiece through repeated sparking. Wire EDM uses a thin wire as an electrode to cut intricate shapes in the workpiece. Hole Drilling EDM creates holes in the workpiece using a rotating tube electrode. Each type of EDM has its specific advantages and is used in various industries for precision machining applications.</p></p>
