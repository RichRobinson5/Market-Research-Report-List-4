<p><strong>The Genome Editing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CRISPR</li><li>TALEN</li><li>ZFN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Genome editing market is categorized into three main types: CRISPR, TALEN, and ZFN. CRISPR, or Clustered Regularly Interspaced Short Palindromic Repeats, is a popular and versatile gene editing tool. TALEN, or Transcription Activator-Like Effector Nucleases, is another widely used method that offers high precision and efficiency. ZFN, or Zinc Finger Nucleases, is an older genome editing technology that has been overshadowed by CRISPR in recent years. Each of these techniques offers unique advantages and applications in the field of genetic engineering.</p></p>
