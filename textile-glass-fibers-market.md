Continuous Fiber is made up of long strands of glass fibers that are woven into various fabrics. Fixed Length Fiber consists of shorter fibers that are used in applications like reinforcement materials. Glass Wool is a type of insulation material made up of fine strands of glass fibers. Each type serves different purposes in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
