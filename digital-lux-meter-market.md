<p><strong>The Digital LUX Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visual LUX Meter</li><li>Photoelectric LUX Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital LUX meter market consists of two main types: visual LUX meters and photoelectric LUX meters. Visual LUX meters are designed to measure light intensity based on human perception, taking into account the sensitivity of the human eye to different wavelengths of light. Photoelectric LUX meters, on the other hand, use photoelectric cells to measure light intensity directly, providing more accurate and consistent readings. Both types of meters are commonly used in various industries such as photography, cinematography, architecture, and agriculture for precise measurement of light levels.</p></p>
