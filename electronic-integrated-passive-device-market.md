<p><strong>The Electronic Integrated Passive Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrostatic Discharge Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Interference Type</li><li>RF-IPD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Integrated Passive Devices (IPDs) are divided into three main market types based on their functions and applications: Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Type, Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) Type, and RF-IPD Type. ESD IPDs protect electronic devices from damage caused by electrostatic discharge. EMI IPDs shield devices from electromagnetic interference, ensuring stable performance. RF-IPDs are designed for high-frequency applications such as wireless communication systems, providing efficient transmission and reception of radio frequency signals. Each market type caters to specific needs in the electronics industry.</p></p>
