<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The FPGA Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flash-based FPGA</li><li>Antifuse-based FPGA</li><li>SRAM-based FPGA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flash-based FPGA, Antifuse-based FPGA, and SRAM-based FPGA are three main types of FPGA modules in the market. Flash-based FPGA offers reprogrammability but has limited performance. Antifuse-based FPGA provides high reliability and security but lacks reconfigurability. SRAM-based FPGA offers high performance and flexibility but requires constant power to maintain programming. Each type caters to different requirements and applications, giving users options to choose the best suited FPGA module for their specific needs.</p></p>
