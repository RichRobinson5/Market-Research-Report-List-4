<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wire Ropes for Cranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point Contact Wire Rope</li><li>Line Contact Wire Rope</li><li>Surface Contact Wire Rope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire ropes for cranes come in three different types based on their contact with the sheave. Point contact wire ropes have one point of contact with the sheave, making them suitable for light-duty applications. Line contact wire ropes have multiple points of contact, providing better load distribution and flexibility. Surface contact wire ropes have full contact with the sheave, offering maximum strength and durability for heavy-duty operations. Each type serves a specific purpose based on the crane's load capacity and application requirements.</p></p>
