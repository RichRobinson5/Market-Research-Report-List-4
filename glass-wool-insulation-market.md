<p><p>Glass wool insulation is available in two main types: produced in rolls and produced in slabs. Glass wool produced in rolls is typically used for insulating walls, floors, and ceilings, providing a flexible and easy-to-install option. On the other hand, glass wool produced in slabs is ideal for insulating larger surfaces such as roofs and external walls. Both types of insulation offer excellent thermal and acoustic properties, making them popular choices for residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>
