<p><strong>The Cassette Air Conditioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Out Wind</li><li>Two Out Wind</li><li>Four Out Wind</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cassette air conditioners come in three market types: Single out wind, Two out wind, and Four out wind. Single out wind cassette air conditioners have one air outlet in the center, providing even airflow in one direction. Two out wind models have two air outlets on opposite sides, allowing for flexible airflow settings in different directions. Four out wind cassette air conditioners have four air outlets on each side, ensuring thorough circulation and coverage in all directions. Each type caters to different preferences and room layouts.</p></p>
