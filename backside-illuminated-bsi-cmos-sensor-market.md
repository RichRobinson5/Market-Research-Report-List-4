<p><p>Backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensor market consists of two main types: Passive Pixel Sensor CMOS and Active Pixel Sensor CMOS. Passive Pixel Sensor CMOS technology is characterized by a simple structure but higher noise levels compared to Active Pixel Sensor CMOS. Meanwhile, Active Pixel Sensor CMOS offers improved performance with on-chip amplification and noise reduction capabilities. Both types cater to different market needs, with Passive Pixel Sensor CMOS suitable for cost-sensitive applications and Active Pixel Sensor CMOS more commonly used in high-performance imaging systems.</p></p>
