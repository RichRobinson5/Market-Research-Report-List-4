<p><p>Radar Target Simulator (RTES) is available in two main types: Benchtop RTES and Portable RTES. Benchtop RTES are typically larger, stationary systems that are ideal for laboratory settings or permanent installations. These systems provide high levels of accuracy and versatility in simulating radar targets. On the other hand, Portable RTES are smaller, more compact systems that can be easily transported and set up for on-site testing or field exercises. They offer flexibility and convenience for users who need to simulate radar targets in different locations.</p></p>
