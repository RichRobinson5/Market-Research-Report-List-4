<p><p>Apparel printing machines are categorized into different types based on the printing technology they use. Heat transfer printing machines use heat to transfer a design onto fabric. Screen printing machines involve pushing ink through a mesh screen onto the fabric. Digital printing machines use computerized technology to print designs directly onto fabric. Other types of apparel printing machines include sublimation printers, which use heat to transfer dye onto fabric. Each type of machine offers unique advantages in terms of quality, speed, and cost-effectiveness for apparel printing processes.</p></p>
