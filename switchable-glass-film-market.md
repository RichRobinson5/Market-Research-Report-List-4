<p><p>Switchable glass film is a versatile product that comes in two main types - self-adhesive film and non-adhesive film. Self-adhesive film features a sticky backing that allows for easy installation on any smooth surface, making it ideal for temporary applications or DIY projects. On the other hand, non-adhesive film relies on static cling technology to adhere to surfaces without any sticky residue, offering a reusable and easy-to-remove option. Both types of switchable glass film provide privacy and light control by transitioning from transparent to opaque with the flick of a switch.</p></p>
