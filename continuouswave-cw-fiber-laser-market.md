<p><p>Continuous wave (CW) fiber lasers are categorized into two main types: single mode and multi mode. Single mode fiber lasers have a narrow beam profile with high coherence, making them ideal for precision applications such as cutting, welding, and medical procedures. Multi mode fiber lasers produce a wider beam profile, making them suitable for applications that require higher power output such as material processing and military applications. Each type of fiber laser has its own advantages and is used in different industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
