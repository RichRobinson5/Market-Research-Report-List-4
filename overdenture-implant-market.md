<p><p>Overdenture implant market consists of two main types: Tooth-Supported Overdentures and Implant-Supported Overdentures. Tooth-Supported Overdentures are supported by remaining natural teeth, providing stability and support for the denture. Implant-Supported Overdentures are anchored to dental implants within the jawbone, offering increased retention and stability compared to traditional dentures. Both types of overdentures offer solutions for patients who have lost multiple teeth and are seeking a more secure and functional option for tooth replacement.</p></p>
