<p><p>In the oral water flossers market, there are two main types of products: constant frequency and frequency conversion. Constant frequency flossers operate at a fixed level of water pressure throughout the flossing process, making them ideal for users who prefer consistency. On the other hand, frequency conversion flossers allow users to adjust the water pressure levels to their preference, providing more control and customization. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in oral hygiene care.</p></p>
