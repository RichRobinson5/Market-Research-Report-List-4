<p><strong>The Accumulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weight-Loaded Piston Type</li><li>Diaphragm (Bladder) Type</li><li>Spring Type</li><li>Hydro-Pneumatic Piston Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Accumulators are used in hydraulic systems to store and release energy. The Weight-Loaded Piston Type accumulator uses weights to apply pressure on the fluid. The Diaphragm (Bladder) Type accumulator uses a flexible bladder to separate gas and fluid. The Spring Type accumulator uses a spring to apply pressure. The Hydro-Pneumatic Piston Type accumulator combines gas and hydraulic fluid to store energy. Each type of accumulator has its benefits and applications, depending on the specific needs of the hydraulic system.</p></p>
