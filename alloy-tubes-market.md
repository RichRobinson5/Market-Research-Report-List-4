<p><ul><li>Seamless Alloy Tubes</li><li>Welded Alloy Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alloy tubes are divided into two main types: seamless alloy tubes and welded alloy tubes. Seamless alloy tubes are made without any welding seams, providing high strength and resistance to corrosion. They are commonly used in high-pressure applications. Welded alloy tubes are made by welding together individual pieces of metal. Although they may be more cost-effective, they are not as strong as seamless tubes and are more susceptible to corrosion. Both types of alloy tubes serve different purposes in various industries.</p></p>
