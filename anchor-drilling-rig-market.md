<p><p>Anchor drilling rigs are commonly used in construction and civil engineering projects. There are three main types of anchor drilling rigs: Crawler Anchor Drilling Rig, Top Driven Anchor Drilling Rig, and Slope Anchor Drilling Rig.</p><p>Crawler anchor drilling rigs are designed for rough terrains and are able to move easily on uneven surfaces. Top Driven anchor drilling rigs use a hydraulic rotary drive system to drill deep into the ground. Slope anchor drilling rigs are specifically designed for drilling on steep slopes or inclines, providing stability and safety during the drilling process.</p></p>
