<p><p>Smart ventilation systems come in three main types: floor embedded, ceiling embedded, and wall embedded. Floor embedded systems have vents located in the floor, often near walls, to allow for efficient air circulation. Ceiling embedded systems have vents located in the ceiling, providing even distribution of air throughout the room. Wall embedded systems have vents located in the walls, allowing for discreet and effective ventilation. Each type offers unique benefits and can be tailored to suit different building designs and layouts.</p></p>
