<p><p>Polishing resin market includes two main types - cationic resin and anionic resin. Cationic resin is positively charged and is used for removing negatively charged ions in the water treatment process. It is effective in polishing water and improving its quality. On the other hand, anionic resin is negatively charged and is used for removing positively charged ions from water. Both types of resins play a vital role in the purification of water by removing impurities and ensuring better quality water for consumption.</p></p>
