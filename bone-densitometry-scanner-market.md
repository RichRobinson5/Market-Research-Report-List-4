<p><strong>The Bone Densitometry Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray</li><li>Ultrasonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bone densitometry scanner market is segmented into X-ray and ultrasonic types. X-ray scanners use low-dose radiation to measure bone mineral density, providing detailed images of the bone structure. Ultrasonic scanners use sound waves to assess bone density, offering a radiation-free alternative for certain patients. Both types are used to diagnose osteoporosis and monitor bone health. The X-ray scanners are more commonly used due to their accuracy and precision, while ultrasonic scanners are preferred for certain patient populations due to their non-invasive nature.</p></p>
