<p><p>Shot blasting machine and sand blasting machine are two types of blasting machines used in the market. Shot blasting machine is used to clean, strengthen, and polish metal surfaces using high-velocity shots of abrasive material. Sand blasting machine, on the other hand, uses compressed air to propel fine sand particles at high speed to clean and etch surfaces. Both machines are commonly used in industries like automotive, aerospace, construction, and metalworking for surface preparation and finishing purposes.</p></p>
