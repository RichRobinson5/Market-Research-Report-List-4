<p><p>The ceramic pigments market can be categorized into two main types based on their composition and properties. The Simple Compound Type consists of pigments made up of basic compounds like oxides, sulfides, and chromates, offering vibrant colors and strong opacity. On the other hand, the Solid Solution-Oxide Type involves pigments with a more complex structure where two or more compounds are mixed at the atomic level, providing a wider range of colors and enhanced stability. Both types cater to different applications in the ceramic industry, offering various benefits to manufacturers.</p></p>
