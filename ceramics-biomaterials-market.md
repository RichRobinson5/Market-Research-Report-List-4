<p><ul><li>Bio-Inert Ceramics</li><li>Bioactive Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramics biomaterials market includes bio-inert ceramics and bioactive ceramics. Bio-inert ceramics are non-reactive materials that do not interact with biological tissues, making them suitable for implants and prosthetics. Bioactive ceramics, on the other hand, have the ability to bond with living tissues, promoting bone growth and integration. These materials are commonly used in dental implants and bone scaffolds. Both types of ceramics play a crucial role in the field of medical biomaterials, offering a range of options for various applications in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
