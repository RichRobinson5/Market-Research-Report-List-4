<p><p>Diesel fuel pumps can be classified into two main types: mechanical fuel pump and electric fuel pump. Mechanical fuel pumps are powered by the engine's motion, delivering fuel to the engine through mechanical means. On the other hand, electric fuel pumps are powered by an electric motor and provide a consistent flow of fuel to the engine. Both types of fuel pumps play a crucial role in delivering fuel to the engine efficiently and ensuring optimal performance of diesel vehicles.</p></p>
