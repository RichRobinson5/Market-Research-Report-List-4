<p><p>Spectroscopic Reflectometry refers to the analysis of materials based on their reflection properties. The market for this technology includes two main types, namely Automatic Spectral Ellipsometers and Imaging Ellipsometers. Automatic Spectral Ellipsometers are used for fast and accurate measurements of thin film thickness and optical constants. On the other hand, Imaging Ellipsometers provide detailed information about the surface properties of materials by mapping the ellipsometric parameters across a sample. Both technologies are important tools in research and industrial applications for material analysis and characterization.</p></p>
