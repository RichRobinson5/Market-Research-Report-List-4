<p><p>Rotational molding, also known as rotomolding, is a manufacturing process used for producing hollow plastic parts. A rotational molding machine is used to heat and rotate a hollow mold, causing the molten material inside to stick to the walls of the mold and form the desired shape. <p><p>There are two main types of rotational moulding machines in the market - Biaxial Rotation Type and Rock and Roll Type. Biaxial Rotation Type machines provide two directions of rotation, allowing for more complex shapes to be produced. Rock and Roll Type machines tilt and rotate the mould during the heating process, ensuring even distribution of the material. Both types offer advantages in different molding applications, catering to the diverse needs of the rotational moulding industry.</p></p>
