<p><p>Bamboo textiles are classified into two main market types: natural bamboo fiber and chemical bamboo fiber. Natural bamboo fiber is produced through mechanical processes that maintain the integrity of the bamboo plant, resulting in a sustainable and eco-friendly fabric. On the other hand, chemical bamboo fiber is manufactured using chemical treatments which break down the bamboo plant to extract the cellulose fibers. While both types of bamboo textiles offer unique advantages, consumers can choose based on their preference for natural or chemically processed materials.</p></p>
