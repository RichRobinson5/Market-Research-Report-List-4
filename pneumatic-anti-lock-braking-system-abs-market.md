<p><p>The Pneumatic Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) market includes components such as Electronic Control Unit (ECU), Solenoid Modulator Valve, and Wheel Speed Sensor. The ECU regulates the ABS system by processing data from sensors. The Solenoid Modulator Valve adjusts the brake pressure to prevent wheel lockup. The Wheel Speed Sensor measures the speed of each wheel, allowing the ABS to monitor and control individual brake pressure. These components work together to improve vehicle stability and prevent skidding during braking.</p></p>
