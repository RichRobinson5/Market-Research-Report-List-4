<p><p>Passive Railway Wheel Sensor Market includes sensors that detect changes in magnetic field as the wheel passes through, typically generating an electrical pulse. These sensors are cost-effective and easy to install. Active Railway Wheel Sensor Market consists of sensors that require an external power source to generate a magnetic field, providing more accurate and reliable data on wheel movements. Though more expensive, active sensors offer higher precision and reliability in detecting wheel rotations and abnormalities.</p></p>
