<p><p>Hydraulic brake fluid is classified into different types based on their composition and properties. DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1 are the common types of brake fluids available in the market. DOT 3 fluids are glycol-based, while DOT 4 fluids have a higher boiling point. DOT 5 fluids are silicone-based and do not absorb water, while DOT 5.1 fluids are high-performance fluids with a high wet boiling point. Each type of brake fluid is designed for specific vehicle requirements and operating conditions.</p></p>
