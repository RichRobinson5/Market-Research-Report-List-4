<p><p>Passenger boarding stairs can be classified into two main types: towable passenger stairways and chassis-mounted passenger stairways. Towable passenger stairways are portable stairs that can be easily towed behind a vehicle to different locations on the airport tarmac. Chassis-mounted passenger stairways are permanently fixed to a chassis or vehicle, providing a stable and reliable platform for passengers to board and disembark from aircraft. Both types of passenger boarding stairs are essential for ensuring safe and efficient boarding processes at airports.</p></p>
