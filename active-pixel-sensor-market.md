<p><p>Active pixel sensors are a type of image sensor that use various market types, including buried photodiode-structure, selection transistor, and MOS amplifier. Buried photodiode-structure APS have a photodiode buried under the pixel circuitry for improved light sensitivity. Selection transistor APS use a transistor to select individual pixels for readout. MOS amplifier APS use metal-oxide-semiconductor transistors to amplify the signal before readout. Each market type offers unique advantages and is used in different applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
