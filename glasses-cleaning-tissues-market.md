<p><p>Glasses cleaning tissues are available in two main types: pre-moistened tissues and dry tissues. Pre-moistened tissues come pre-soaked with a cleaning solution, making them convenient for on-the-go use. They are great for quickly removing dust, fingerprints, and smudges from glasses. Dry tissues, on the other hand, are designed to be used with a separate cleaning solution. They are ideal for more thorough cleaning and are often used in combination with a spray cleaner for a streak-free finish on glasses.</p></p>
