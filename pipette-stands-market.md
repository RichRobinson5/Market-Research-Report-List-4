<p><p>Pipette stands are available in two main types- rotating and fixed. Rotating pipette stands have a carousel design that allows users to easily spin the stand to access different pipettes. They are ideal for busy labs where quick access to multiple pipettes is crucial. On the other hand, fixed pipette stands have a stationary design and are suitable for labs with limited space or where pipettes need to be arranged in a specific order. Both types offer convenient storage and organization for pipettes.</p></p>
