<p><p>Mono Turbo refers to a type of turbocharger system that includes a single turbocharger unit on the engine, increasing power output and efficiency. On the other hand, Twin Turbo involves two turbochargers working together to enhance engine performance. Mono Turbo is more commonly used in smaller vehicles due to cost-effectiveness, while Twin Turbo is typically found in high-performance vehicles for greater power and acceleration. Both systems aim to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions in new energy vehicles.</p></p>
