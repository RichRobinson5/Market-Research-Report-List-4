<p><p>Traffic management market consists of various systems designed to optimize traffic flow in urban areas. Urban Traffic Management and Control (UTMC) system uses sensors and cameras to monitor and adjust traffic signals in real-time. Adaptive Traffic Control System (ATCS) utilizes data to dynamically adjust signal timing. Journey Time Measurement System (JTMS) collects data to improve travel time estimates. Predictive Traffic Modeling System (PTMS) predicts traffic patterns. Incident Detection and Location System (IDLS) quickly identifies and locates traffic incidents. Dynamic Traffic Management System (DTMS) combines various systems for comprehensive traffic management.</p></p>
