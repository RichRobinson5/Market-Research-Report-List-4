<p><ul><li>Ceramic Core Wire-winding Inductor</li><li>Magnetic Core Wire-winding Inductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire winding inductors are commonly categorized into two main types based on the core material used: ceramic core wire-winding inductors and magnetic core wire-winding inductors. Ceramic core wire-winding inductors utilize a ceramic material for the core, providing high temperature stability and low core loss. On the other hand, magnetic core wire-winding inductors use materials such as iron or ferrite for the core, offering high magnetic permeability and efficient energy storage. Both types of inductors have their unique advantages and are used in various applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
