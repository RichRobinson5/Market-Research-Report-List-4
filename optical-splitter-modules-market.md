<p><strong>The Optical splitter Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters (FBT)</li><li>Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters (PLC)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical splitter modules are devices used in fiber optic communication systems to split incoming optical signals into multiple output signals. The three main types of optical splitter modules are Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters (FBT), which use a fused tapering technique to split signals; Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters (PLC), which use a planar waveguide structure with a built-in splitter circuit; and other types of splitters in the market, which may include Micro Optics, Tapered Waveguides, and Arrayed Waveguide Gratings. Each type of splitter offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
