<p><p>Intelligent RTUs are categorized into two main market types: Wireless RTUs and Wired RTUs. Wireless RTUs utilize wireless communication technologies such as cellular or satellite connections to transmit data, providing flexibility and scalability in remote monitoring and control applications. Wired RTUs, on the other hand, rely on physical connections like Ethernet or serial cables for data transmission, offering a more secure and stable communication method. Both Wireless and Wired RTUs play a crucial role in various industries by enabling efficient and reliable monitoring and control of remote assets.</p></p>
