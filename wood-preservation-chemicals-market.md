<p><p>Wood Preservation Chemicals market can be divided into three main types based on the type of preservatives used in the treatment process. Water-borne Preservatives are chemicals diluted in water and are commonly used for outdoor wood applications. Oil-borne Preservatives are chemicals mixed with oil and provide better protection against fungi and insects. Light Organic Solvent Preservatives are chemicals dissolved in solvents like acetone or ethanol, and are known for their fast drying time and effectiveness in treating wood.</p></p>
