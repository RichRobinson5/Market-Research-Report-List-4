<p><p>In-sink disposal market is categorized into two main types: Batch Feed Type and Continuous Feed Type. Batch feed disposals require users to load food waste into the unit before turning it on, providing a safer and more controlled operation. On the other hand, Continuous feed disposals allow users to continuously add waste while the unit is running, offering more convenience and faster operation. These two types cater to different consumer preferences and needs in the market for in-sink disposals.</p></p>
