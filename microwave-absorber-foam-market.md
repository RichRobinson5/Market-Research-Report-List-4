<p><ul><li>Low Permeability</li><li>High Permeability</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microwave absorber foam is divided into two main types based on permeability: low permeability and high permeability. Low permeability foam is designed to absorb microwaves efficiently and reduce their reflection, making it suitable for applications where minimal signal loss is desired. On the other hand, high permeability foam allows for the easy penetration of microwaves, making it ideal for applications where high absorption rates are necessary. Both types of foam play a crucial role in managing electromagnetic interference and improving signal performance in various industries.</p></p>
