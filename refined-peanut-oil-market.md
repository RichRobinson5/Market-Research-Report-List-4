<p><p>Refined peanut oil can be categorized into two main types: cold pressed and hot pressed. Cold pressed peanut oil is extracted at low temperatures, which helps retain more of the oil's natural flavor and nutrients. On the other hand, hot pressed peanut oil is produced using high temperatures, resulting in a higher yield but potentially compromising some of the oil's quality. Both types are widely used in cooking, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical industries for their unique properties and health benefits.</p></p>
