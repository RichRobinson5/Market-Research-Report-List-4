<p><strong>The Refractor Telescope Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concave</li><li>Convex</li><li>Concave-convex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refractor telescope lenses come in three main types: concave, convex, and concave-convex. Concave lenses are thicker at the edges than in the center and diverge incoming light rays. Convex lenses are thicker in the center and thinner at the edges, converging incoming light rays. Concave-convex lenses combine the qualities of both concave and convex lenses, allowing for better manipulation of light rays. Each type has its own unique properties and applications in refractor telescope technology.</p></p>
