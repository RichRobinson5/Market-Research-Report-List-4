The ISFET Sensor, on the other hand, uses ion-sensitive field-effect transistors for sensing. Other types of sensors in the market include combination electrodes, electrodeless sensors, and solid-state sensors. Each type of sensor has its unique advantages and applications in monitoring the ORP levels in various industries.</p></p>
