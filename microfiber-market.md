<p><p>Microfiber market can be categorized into two main types based on the length of the fibers: long microfiber and short microfiber. Long microfiber refers to fibers with a length greater than 112 µm, while short microfiber refers to fibers with a length less than 100 µm. Long microfiber is often used in applications where durability and strength are important, such as automotive upholstery and industrial cleaning cloths. Short microfiber, on the other hand, is commonly used in applications where softness and absorbency are key, such as in clothing and bedding.</p></p>
