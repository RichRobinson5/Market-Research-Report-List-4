<p><p>Protocol Analyser Market Types such as Vector Network Analyser and Scalar Network Analyser play a crucial role in network testing and analysis. Vector Network Analysers are mainly used for measuring complex impedance, S-parameters, and reflection coefficient of high-frequency circuits. On the other hand, Scalar Network Analysers are used for measuring magnitude and phase of circuit parameters. Both types of analysers help in identifying issues in network performance, optimizing network design, and ensuring proper functioning of communication systems.</p></p>
