<p><ul><li>Zero Emission System</li><li>Closed Loop System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zero Emission System in the mud handling equipment market refers to systems that aim to minimize or completely eliminate emissions of pollutants and greenhouse gases during the handling of drilling mud. This is achieved through the use of advanced technologies such as electric motors and renewable energy sources. On the other hand, Closed Loop System involves reusing drilling mud within the system, reducing the need for fresh mud and minimizing waste generation. Both types of systems are geared towards reducing environmental impact and promoting sustainability in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
