<p><p>Drilling and foundation equipment market consists of various types, including Kelly drilling, continuous flight auger drilling, double rotary drilling, and others. Kelly drilling involves the use of a telescopic Kelly bar to drill deep foundation piles. Continuous flight auger drilling uses a continuous spiral flighted auger to remove soil as the drill advances. Double rotary drilling utilizes two rotating drill bits for increased efficiency. Other types of equipment in the market include micropile drilling and soldier pile drilling among others.</p></p>
