<p><p>Wired occupancy sensors come in various types based on technology and coverage area. Types based on technology include Ultrasonic, Infrared (IR), and Ultrasonic + Passive Infrared (PIR) sensors. Ultrasonic sensors emit high-frequency sound waves, while IR sensors detect motion based on heat signatures. Ultrasonic + PIR sensors combine both technologies for more accurate detection. Coverage area options include less than 89°, 90-179°, and 180-360°. Ultrasonic + PIR sensors in the market offer a comprehensive solution for detecting occupancy accurately in various spaces.</p></p>
