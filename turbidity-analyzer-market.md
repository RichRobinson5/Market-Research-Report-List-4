<p><p>Turbidity analyzers are used to measure the clarity of water by analyzing the amount of suspended particles present. The market for turbidity analyzers can be divided into two types: dry media and wet media. Dry media turbidity analyzers use infrared light to measure the amount of scattered light, while wet media analyzers use a probe immersed in the water sample. Both types of analyzers provide accurate and reliable measurements of turbidity levels in various applications such as wastewater treatment, drinking water monitoring, and industrial processes.</p></p>
