<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airport Biometric Market can be categorized into two types: Contact and Contactless. </p><p>Contact biometric systems require physical contact between the user and the device, such as fingerprint scanners or hand geometry readers. These systems are traditionally used for higher security applications.</p><p>On the other hand, Contactless biometric systems do not require physical contact and can be used at a distance, such as facial recognition or iris scanners. These systems are often preferred for their convenience and hygiene in high traffic environments like airports.</p></p>
