<p><p>Photomask repair equipment market types include laser technology, focused ion beam (FIB) technology, and nanomachining technology. Laser technology uses laser beams to repair defects on photomasks. Focused ion beam (FIB) technology uses a focused beam of ions to remove or deposit materials on the photomask surface. Nanomachining technology involves the use of nanoscale tools to repair defects with high precision. These technologies play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and functionality of photomasks used in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
