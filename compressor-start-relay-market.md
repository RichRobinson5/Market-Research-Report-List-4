<p><p>Compressor Start Relay market includes two main types: Heavy Hammer Start Relay and PTC Start Relay. Heavy Hammer Start Relay is designed for high-power compressors, delivering a strong start-up current to kickstart the compressor motor. On the other hand, PTC Start Relay is known for its self-resetting properties and is commonly used in smaller compressors. Both types play crucial roles in controlling the starting process of compressors, ensuring efficient and reliable performance in various applications.</p></p>
