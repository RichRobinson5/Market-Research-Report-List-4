<p><p>Commercial vehicle tailpipes come in two main types: single tailpipe and double tailpipes. Single tailpipe type is commonly found in smaller commercial vehicles and cars, with exhaust gases exiting through a single pipe at the back of the vehicle. Double tailpipes type is typically seen in larger commercial vehicles and performance cars, where two exhaust pipes are used to improve engine efficiency and give the vehicle a more aggressive appearance. Both types play a crucial role in releasing exhaust gases away from the vehicle to maintain optimal engine performance and reduce pollution.</p></p>
