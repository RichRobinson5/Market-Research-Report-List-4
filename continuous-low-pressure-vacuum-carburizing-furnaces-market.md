<p><p>Continuous Low Pressure Vacuum Carburizing Furnaces are available in three main types: Single Chamber, Double Chamber, and Multi Chamber. Single Chamber furnaces are designed for continuous processing of one part at a time. Double Chamber furnaces have two chambers for simultaneous processing of two parts. Multi Chamber furnaces have multiple chambers for processing multiple parts simultaneously. Each type offers unique advantages based on production capacity, efficiency, and flexibility, catering to different manufacturing needs in the market.</p></p>
