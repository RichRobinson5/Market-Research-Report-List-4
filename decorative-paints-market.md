<p><strong>The Decorative Paints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Decorative paints market is divided into two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based paints use chemicals to dissolve pigments and binders, creating a durable and long-lasting finish. However, they emit harmful VOCs and have a strong odor. On the other hand, water-based paints use water as a solvent, making them environmentally friendly with low VOC emissions. They dry quickly and are easy to clean up with soap and water. Both types have their own advantages and are popular choices in the decorative paints market.</p></p>
