<p><p>Brachytherapy catheters are used for delivering radiation therapy directly to a tumor. Single Lumen Balloon Catheters (SLB) have a single lumen for delivering the radiation dose, while Multi Lumen Balloon Catheters (MLB) have multiple lumens for precise delivery of radiation to different areas of the tumor. SLBs are simpler and easier to use, while MLBs offer more versatility and precision in treatment. Both types of catheters play a crucial role in delivering effective brachytherapy treatment to cancer patients.</p></p>
