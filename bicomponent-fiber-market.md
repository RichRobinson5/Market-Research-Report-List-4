<p><p>Bicomponent fibers are classified into various types based on the materials used in their composition. Some common types include PE/PP, PE/PET, and Co-PET/PET. In PE/PP fibers, polyethylene and polypropylene are used as the two components. PE/PET fibers consist of polyethylene and polyethylene terephthalate. Co-PET/PET fibers combine copolyester and polyester materials. Each type of bicomponent fiber offers unique properties and applications, making them suitable for a wide range of industries such as textiles, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
