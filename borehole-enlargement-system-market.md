<p><strong>The Borehole Enlargement System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-diameter Reamers</li><li>Hydraulically Expandable Reamers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Borehole enlargement systems come in two main types: fixed-diameter reamers and hydraulically expandable reamers. Fixed-diameter reamers are designed to maintain a constant hole size, providing stability and accuracy during drilling operations. On the other hand, hydraulically expandable reamers allow for the adjustment of hole size during operation to accommodate changing drilling conditions. Both types of systems are essential tools in the drilling industry, offering versatility and efficiency in borehole enlargement processes.</p></p>
