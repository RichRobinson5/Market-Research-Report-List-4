<p><strong>The Refrigeration Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous</li><li>Intermittent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refrigeration dryers in the continuous market type operate consistently to remove moisture from compressed air without interruption. They are designed for continuous usage and are ideal for applications requiring a constant supply of dry air. In contrast, intermittent refrigeration dryers are utilized in applications where the demand for compressed air fluctuates. They operate based on the preset intervals, cycling on and off as needed to remove moisture from the air. Both types of refrigeration dryers are essential for maintaining the quality of compressed air in various industrial processes.</p></p>
