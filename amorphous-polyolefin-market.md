<p><p>Amorphous Polyolefin (APO) is a type of polymer with various market types including Propylene Homopolymer, Copolymer of Propylene and Ethylene, Propylene Homopolymer and Ethylene Copolymer, and Others. Propylene Homopolymer refers to a polymer composed solely of propylene, while Copolymer of Propylene and Ethylene consists of both propylene and ethylene. Propylene Homopolymer and Ethylene Copolymer is a combination of propylene and ethylene in a specific ratio. The Others category includes APOs with different compositions and properties than the other three market types.</p></p>
