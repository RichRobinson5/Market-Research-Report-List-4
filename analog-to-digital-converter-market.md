<p><p>Analog to Digital Converter (ADC) market consists of various types such as Display ADC, Delta-sigma ADC, Pipelined ADC, and Dual Slope ADC. Display ADCs provide visual output of the converted signal, while Delta-sigma ADCs offer high resolution by oversampling. Pipelined ADCs use multiple stages for faster conversion speeds. Dual Slope ADCs utilize an integrator to balance voltage levels. Each type caters to different needs and specifications, providing options for efficient analog to digital signal conversion in various applications.</p></p>
