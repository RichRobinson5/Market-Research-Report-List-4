<p><p>Macro, Small, Pico, and Femto base stations are the different types of 5G cellular base stations that cater to varying coverage and capacity needs. Macro base stations cover large geographic areas and serve a high number of users. Small base stations are deployed to enhance coverage in urban areas. Pico base stations are designed for indoor coverage in public spaces or offices. Femto base stations are small, low-power stations used for providing coverage in residential areas. Each type serves a unique market segment based on coverage requirements.</p></p>
