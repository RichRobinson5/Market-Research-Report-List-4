<p><p>Hot water unit heaters are commonly categorized into three types based on their design and installation method: Horizontal Type, Vertical Type, and Suspended Type.</p><p>Horizontal Type unit heaters are typically mounted on the wall or ceiling and blow warm air in a horizontal direction. Vertical Type unit heaters are designed to be installed upright and discharge heat vertically.
