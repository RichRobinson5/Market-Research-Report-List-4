<p><p>Ferrochrome alloy market consists of various types based on carbon content. High carbon ferrochrome contains over 4% carbon, medium carbon ferrochrome contains 1-4% carbon, low carbon ferrochrome contains less than 0.5% carbon, and micro-carbon ferrochrome contains trace amounts of carbon. Each type is used in different applications based on their carbon content and properties. High carbon ferrochrome is used in stainless steel production, medium carbon ferrochrome is used in specialty steel alloys, low carbon ferrochrome is used in welding electrodes, and micro-carbon ferrochrome is used in superalloys.</p></p>
