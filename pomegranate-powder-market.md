<p><p>Pomegranate powder is available in two main types in the market - spray drying pomegranate powder and freeze-dried pomegranate powder. Spray drying involves turning liquid pomegranate juice into powder form through a drying process. Freeze-dried pomegranate powder, on the other hand, is created by freezing the juice and then removing the moisture through sublimation. Both types of pomegranate powder retain the nutritional benefits of fresh pomegranates and can be used in a variety of applications such as food and beverage products.</p></p>
