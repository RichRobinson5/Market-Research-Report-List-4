<p><p>Infrared heat lamps are categorized into three types based on the wavelength of the emitted light: Near Infrared lamps have shorter wavelengths and are mostly used for therapeutic purposes such as pain relief and muscle recovery. Medium Infrared lamps have slightly longer wavelengths and are commonly used for heating applications in industrial settings. Far Infrared lamps have the longest wavelengths and are often used in saunas and heating pads for deep tissue penetration and health benefits. Each type of lamp offers unique advantages and applications in different market segments.</p></p>
