<p><p>Stand-Alone Proximity Reader: Stand-alone proximity card readers are independent devices that can be installed at entry points for access control. They are typically used in small to medium-sized businesses where a centralized access control system may not be necessary. These readers are easy to install and operate, providing a simple solution for controlling access to specific areas within a building.</p><p>Wall Switch Proximity Reader: Wall switch proximity card readers are designed to replace traditional light switches with a secure access control solution. These readers are typically used in commercial buildings or offices where access to certain areas needs to be restricted. By integrating access control into a common household fixture, wall switch proximity readers provide a convenient and discreet way to manage entry to secure areas.</p></p>
