<p><p>The Bolt Tension Monitor market includes various types of monitoring methods, with two key categories being Ultrasonic Testing and Sensor Testing. Ultrasonic Testing involves using high-frequency sound waves to detect changes in bolt tension, allowing for non-destructive assessment of the integrity of the bolts. Sensor Testing, on the other hand, utilizes sensors to measure the tension in bolts by detecting changes in pressure or strain. Both methods are crucial for ensuring the safety and reliability of bolted connections in various industries.</p></p>
