<p><p>The metallic foil market can be categorized into hot stamping foil and cold stamping foil. Hot stamping foil is applied using heat and pressure, creating a shiny and reflective finish on various surfaces. Cold stamping foil, on the other hand, does not require heat and is typically used for more delicate materials like paper and plastic. Both types of foils are popular choices for adding a luxurious and decorative touch to products in industries such as packaging, printing, and manufacturing.</p></p>
