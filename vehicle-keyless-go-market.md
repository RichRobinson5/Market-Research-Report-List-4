<p><p>The Vehicle Keyless Go market includes two main types - Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). </p><p>PKES allows the driver to unlock and start the vehicle without having to physically interact with the key fob, as long as it is in close proximity to the vehicle. </p><p>RKES, on the other hand, requires the driver to press a button on the key fob to unlock or start the vehicle from a distance. Both systems offer convenience and security features for vehicle owners.</p></p>
