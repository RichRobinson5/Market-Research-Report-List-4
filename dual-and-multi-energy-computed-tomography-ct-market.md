<p><p>Dual-energy CT uses two different energy levels in order to differentiate various materials in the body, providing more accurate imaging and diagnosis. This technology is further divided into two types: dual-source CT, which uses two X-ray tubes to simultaneously produce images, and rapid kVp switching, which rapidly alternates between two energy settings with a single X-ray tube. Both types allow for enhanced imaging capabilities, leading to more precise diagnosis and treatment planning in medical imaging.</p></p>
