<p><strong>The Three axis Gyroscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interferometric Gyroscope</li><li>Resonant Gyroscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interferometric Gyroscope utilizes the interference pattern created by a split laser beam to measure the rotation rate. This type of gyroscope offers high precision and accuracy, making it ideal for applications requiring precise motion tracking. On the other hand, Resonant Gyroscope operates by using a vibrating mechanical structure to detect changes in orientation. It is known for its high sensitivity and stability, making it suitable for navigation systems and inertial measurement units. Both technologies play a crucial role in various industries, including aerospace, defense, and automotive.</p></p>
