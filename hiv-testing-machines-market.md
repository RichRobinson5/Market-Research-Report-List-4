<p><p>HIV Testing Machines market offers two main types of testing methods: Rapid Immunoassay Test and Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Test. Rapid Immunoassay Test involves detecting antibodies produced by the body in response to HIV infection, providing quick results in a matter of minutes. On the other hand, Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Test detects the genetic material of the virus itself, offering more accurate results and faster turnaround times compared to traditional testing methods. Both types cater to different needs in the HIV testing market, providing options for healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
