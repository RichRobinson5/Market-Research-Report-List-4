<p><p>A dental curing lamp is a device used in dentistry to polymerize composite materials used for dental restorations and various dental treatments. Curing lamps are essential tools in modern dentistry as they allow for quick and efficient curing of dental materials, resulting in durable and long-lasting restorations.</p><p>The dental curing lamp market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of dental disorders and the rising demand for cosmetic dental procedures.
