<p><p>Corrugated Finned Tubes come in two main types: Single Metal Finned Tubes and Bimetal Composite Finned Tubes. Single Metal Finned Tubes are made from a single type of metal, providing uniform heat transfer efficiency. On the other hand, Bimetal Composite Finned Tubes combine two different metals to enhance heat transfer capabilities. This allows for better heat exchange between fluids flowing inside the tube and the surrounding environment. Each type caters to specific applications where unique heat transfer requirements need to be met.</p></p>
