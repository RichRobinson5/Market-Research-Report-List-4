<p><p>A Monorail Light Crane System consists of a single track on which the crane moves horizontally, allowing for efficient material handling in limited spaces. On the other hand, a Double-rail Light Crane System has two parallel tracks, enabling the crane to carry heavier loads and providing increased stability. Both types of systems are commonly used in industrial settings such as warehouses, factories, and assembly lines to streamline operations and improve productivity.</p></p>
