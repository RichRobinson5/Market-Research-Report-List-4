<p><p>The Automotive VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) Battery market includes two main types - AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) Battery and Gel Battery. AGM batteries use a absorbent glass mat separator to hold the electrolyte in place, offering good performance and reliability. Gel batteries use a silica gel to immobilize the electrolyte, making them maintenance-free and resistant to vibration. Both types of batteries are commonly used in automotive applications for their durability and efficiency.</p></p>
