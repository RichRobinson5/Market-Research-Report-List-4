<p><p>H2S scavenger market can be categorized into amine-based and triazine-based products, as well as non-amine-based materials. Amine-based products typically use chemicals like MEA or MDEA to chemically react with H2S and remove it from gas streams. Triazine-based products work in a similar manner but use different compounds like DEA or TEA. Non-amine-based materials employ alternative mechanisms, such as oxidation or adsorption, to remove H2S. Each type of scavenger has its own advantages and applications in various industries such as oil and gas.</p></p>
