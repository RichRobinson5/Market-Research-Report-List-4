<p><p>Wet electrolyte tantalum capacitors are divided into two main market types: surface mount tantalum capacitors and radial lead type tantalum capacitors. Surface mount tantalum capacitors are mounted directly onto the surface of a circuit board, making them more compact and efficient for modern electronic devices. Radial lead type tantalum capacitors, on the other hand, have leads that extend from the body of the capacitor, allowing for easy installation in through-hole circuit board applications. Both types offer high capacitance and voltage ratings, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
