<p><p>High Voltage Induction Motors are commonly classified into two types: Squirrel-Cage and Slip-Ring motors. Squirrel-Cage motors are the most widely used, known for their simple design and maintenance-free operation. They are ideal for applications requiring constant speed, such as pumps and fans. Slip-Ring motors, on the other hand, offer more flexibility in controlling speed and torque, making them suitable for applications like hoists and cranes. Both types play a crucial role in various industrial sectors due to their high efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
