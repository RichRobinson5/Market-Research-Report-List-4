<p><ul><li>Continuous Blood Purification (CBP)</li><li>Hemodialysis (HD)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Kidney Machines market consists of three main types: Continuous Blood Purification (CBP), Hemodialysis (HD), and other market segments. CBP involves a method of continuous filtration to remove waste and excess fluids from the blood. HD, on the other hand, involves the use of a machine to filter and clean the blood outside the body before returning it back to the patient. The "other" market segment may include emerging technologies or alternative methods for kidney treatment beyond CBP and HD.</p></p>
