<p><ul><li>Hot Plating</li><li>Electrophoresis</li><li>Vacuum Evaporation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminised steel sheet market types include hot plating, electrophoresis, and vacuum evaporation. Hot plating involves immersing the steel sheet in a bath of molten aluminum to create a protective coating. Electrophoresis is a process where an electric current is used to deposit aluminum particles onto the steel sheet. Vacuum evaporation involves evaporating aluminum in a vacuum chamber and then depositing it onto the steel sheet. These methods all contribute to enhancing the durability and corrosion resistance of the steel sheet.</p></p>
