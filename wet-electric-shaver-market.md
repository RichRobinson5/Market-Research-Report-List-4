<p><p>There are two main types of wet electric shavers available in the market: rotary shavers and reciprocating shavers. Rotary shavers have circular heads with spinning blades that move in a circular motion, providing a close and comfortable shave. Reciprocating shavers, on the other hand, have straight blades that move back and forth rapidly to cut the hairs. Each type of shaver offers a different shaving experience, with users having preferences based on factors like skin sensitivity, hair texture, and shaving habits.</p></p>
