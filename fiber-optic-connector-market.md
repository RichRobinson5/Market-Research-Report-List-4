<p><p>Fiber Optic Connectors are essential components in optical communication systems. The FC Connector, SC Connector, LC Connector, and ST Connector are the most commonly used types in the market. The FC Connector features a screw-on design for secure connections, while the SC Connector offers easy push-pull coupling. The LC Connector is popular for its small form factor, and the ST Connector is known for its bayonet-style locking mechanism. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the fiber optic communication industry.</p></p>
