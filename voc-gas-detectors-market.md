Fixed VOC Gas Detectors are installed in a specific location to continuously monitor and detect volatile organic compounds in the air. They are commonly used in industrial settings, refineries, and manufacturing plants. On the other hand, Portable VOC Gas Detectors are handheld devices that can be carried around for on-the-go detection of VOCs. They are ideal for workers who need to monitor their exposure to harmful gases in different locations. Each type serves a specific purpose in ensuring safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
