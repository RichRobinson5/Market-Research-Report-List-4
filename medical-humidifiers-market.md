<p><p>Medical humidifiers can be classified into three main types: bubble humidifiers, electronic humidifiers, and ultrasonic humidifiers. Bubble humidifiers use air flow to create bubbles that provide moisture to the air. Electronic humidifiers control humidity levels electronically, ensuring precise and consistent moisture output. Ultrasonic humidifiers use ultrasonic vibrations to produce a fine mist of water particles, effectively increasing humidity levels. Each type of humidifier has its own advantages and is suitable for different medical applications based on specific needs.</p></p>
